American Institute of Chemical Engineers (AIChE)

The American Institute of Chemical Engineers is a professional organization created to further the field of chemical engineering. Our UC Berkeley student chapter serves to help ChemE students in their journey through college. Students in any year with an interest in pursuing chemical engineering can join, and we highly encourage you to take advantage of our resources! We host many different events throughout the year, ranging from socials and outreach opportunities to company-specific infosessions and dinners with members of the Bay Area startup community. You can become more hands-on by joining one of our committees (Social, Corporate, Outreach, or Media) and help plan out these events through weekly meetings - we typically accept new committee applicants every semester! To get involved, check us out on Facebook or our website and look out for future events!
