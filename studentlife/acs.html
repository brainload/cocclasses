American Chemical Society at Berkeley



ACS at Berkeley is the student chapter of the American Chemical Society at UC Berkeley. We serve as a connection between students at UC Berkeley and the local and national ACS organizations. Our goal is to provide academic and professional resources for chemistry, chemical biology, and chemical engineering students through info-sessions, workshops, panels, talks, networking events, and mentorship. In addition, we also promote chemistry throughout our local community through our education and outreach efforts at local elementary schools and community colleges. Through these efforts, we hope to offer a network and community for students interested in chemistry.
