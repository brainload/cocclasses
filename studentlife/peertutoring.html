Peer Tutoring

The undergraduate Peer Tutoring Program aims to serve College of Chemistry students through accessible, reliable, and talented peer tutors. Tutoring takes place in the newly renovated Peer Tutoring and Study Center in Bixby Commons, with tutors available from 10am-5pm on weekdays.

To see a detailed schedule and view a list of tutors, visit this link.


Peer Services Mission Statement:

The College of Chemistry is committed to providing comprehensive academic support for all students in the College. Our mission is to enhance the academic performance and well-being of all College of Chemistry students through the service of vetted peer advisors and talented peer tutors capable of providing sound student perspectives and insight. We aim for the students in our programs to build the discipline and confidence necessary to become lifelong learners, dedicated scientists, and world-class leaders.
