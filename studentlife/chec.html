Chec Consulting

Chec Consulting is a student organization at UC Berkeley that provides pro-bono professional technical consulting to startups and established industry leaders. The organization serves to facilitate an environment in which students can grow professionally while gaining valuable experience in technical consulting. Our consultants have a variety of STEM backgrounds, including chemical engineering, bioengineering, chemistry, computer science, and data visualization.
