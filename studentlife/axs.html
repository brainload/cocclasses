Alpha Chi Sigma

Alpha Chi Sigma is a professional chemistry fraternity at the University of California, Berkeley. Being a co-ed organization, we encourage all students at the UC Berkeley to apply if they have taken one previous chemistry course, including AP Chemistry, and are interested in pursuing the chemical sciences. We are a tight-knit community of students with weekly meetings on Thursday nights as well as tutoring, professional, social and community service events throughout the semester. Such events include our annual Research Symposium in the spring and going on retreats to places like Lake Tahoe, Sequoia National Park, or Lake Shasta. Look for us tabeling in the College of Chemistry plaza and come out to out rush events the first full week of school. More information can be found on our website: axs.berkeley.edu!
